AUTHENTIC MEXICAN CUISINE

Cochinita Pibil

EXPERIENCE OUR EXQUISITE COCHINITA TACOS

Discover the taste of tradition with our Cochinita Pibil, a slow-roasted Mayan pork dish from the Yucatán Peninsula. Marinated in citrus juice, annatto seed, and roasted in a píib wrapped in banana leaf. Served with pickles, onions, and green salsa, it's perfect over rice or in a taco.

CHICHARRON

TASTE OUR DELICIOUS CHICHARRON TACOS

Indulge in the timeless delight of Classic Chicharron, deep-fried pork skin, typically sourced from pork belly. Seasoned to perfection and traditionally fried in its own lard, the result is a crispy and flavorful treat. Our Chicharron is accompanied by chipotle salsa, fresh cilantro, and onions. Transform your experience with Chicharron Carnitas, a popular choice for crafting mouthwatering tacos.

"I wanted to bring authentic interior-Mexican cuisine from my homeland"

- Mariam O'donnell

Mariam O’Donnell (Founder/Owner) is a graduate of the Le Cordon Bleu College of Culinary Arts in Austin, TX. Mariam was driven to bring authentic interior-Mexican cuisine from her homeland. From this, La Kochina was born.

Our service includes specialties ranging from Cochinita Pibil, Chicharon, homemade salsas, guacamole, and desserts. We provide catering for small and large events with partial or full service options. Delivery is available.
